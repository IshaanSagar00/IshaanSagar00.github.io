Frequently Asked Questions

What is an EMI?

EMI (Equated Monthly Installment) is the fixed amount you pay every month to repay a loan, including principal and interest.

How is EMI calculated?

EMI is calculated using loan amount, interest rate, and loan tenure using a standard formula followed by banks in India.

Is this EMI calculator free?

Yes, this EMI calculator is completely free and requires no registration.

Does this calculator store my data?

No. All calculations are done in your browser. No personal or financial data is stored.

Is the EMI amount accurate?

Yes. The calculation matches standard bank EMI formulas. Final EMI may vary slightly based on lender charges.
